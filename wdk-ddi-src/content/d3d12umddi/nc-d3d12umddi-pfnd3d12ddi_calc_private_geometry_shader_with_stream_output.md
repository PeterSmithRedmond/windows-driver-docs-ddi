## -description

A handle to the display device (graphics context).

### -param unnamedParam2

Pointer to a D3D12DDIARG_CREATE_GEOMETRY_SHADER_WITH_STREAM_OUTPUT structure.

### -param unnamedParam3

Pointer to a D3D12DDIARG_STAGE_IO_SIGNATURES structure.

Returns SIZE_T.
